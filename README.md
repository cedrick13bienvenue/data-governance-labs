## Overview

This repository contains completed deliverables for three Data Governance lab assignments. All legal analysis, compliance obligations, penalties, and data subject rights are interpreted through **Rwanda Law No. 058/2021** unless a customer's jurisdiction requires a different applicable law (GDPR for EU residents, CCPA/CPRA for California residents).

## Labs Summary

| # | Lab | Scenario | Key Skill |
|---|-----|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **MedTrack Data Quality Detective** | Patient appointment database with six data quality dimension violations causing SMS failures, billing errors, and duplicate records | Data Quality Assessment |
| 2 | **ShopGhana Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance** | Three simultaneous data deletion requests from customers in Rwanda, Germany, and California | Multi-jurisdiction data subject rights |
| 3 | **QuickLoan Governance Review** | Fintech ML loan-scoring pipeline with data quality, legal compliance, and algorithmic bias failures | Governance Review Card + Corrected Data Flow |
